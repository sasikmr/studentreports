Overview & definitions

  • Time Spent: This is an estimate of the total time a student  have spent on the application. This takes into account the time spent for through feeds read , questionnaires answered and several others modules inside the application. 
    •  
  • Learning: Learning is quantified in this report based on how much information student accept from the platform and how much he can reproduce during assessments. This takes in to account the academic scores, questionnaire scores, feeds he/she read, various levels of challenges taken, study materials used and the time spent in questionnaire modules. There is a unique edwisely learning model derived from the rigorous research on the heuristics learning and standard stotastic models that takes weightages for various aspects and produces a unique score in assessing learning of a student. 
    •  
  • Technology Utilization: It is a measure of your overall engagement of a student/ faculty in the edwisely technology throughout the education. It takes in to accounts feeds read, questionnaire/surveys answered, comments or responses to the posts of the faculty, overral time spent of the product etc., This explains the how much the user has been focusing various modules of technology for his engagement in learning. 
    •  
  • Responses: Response or the response rate represents the percentage of the questionnaires / Surveys / materials answered or used out of many that were sent to the students. This shows that attentiveness that students have towards the posts that they were receiving from the college and the rate at which responded. 
    •  
  • Academics: Academic represent the scoring of the students in the exams conducted by the university or the colleges that he is enrolled in to. This is a conventional assessments methodology for assessing students learning. The results of these academics is obtained from the university or the colleges directly.
    •  
  • Questionnaire: This represents the innovative learning and assessments methodology conducted within the mobile application for all individual units in every subject. The content for these unique questionnaires is sourced from various subject matter experts. This consists of Multiple choice questions that can be automatically forwards to the students from the faculty mobile application. Automatic grading and student understanding level for individual units and overall subject is easily measured from the questionnaire scores.
    •  
  • Feeds: Its a unique and personalized byte sized content, covering interesting and industry relevant content in various fields. These ae deliver to the all the students and faculty with in the mobile application. These cards will be pushed to the application based on the interests of the users and the field he is enrolled in.
    •  
  • Survey: These are the feedbacks that were sent to the students, that would help the faculty or the college to improve the services or the policies. Feedbacks that are conducted digital gives that clear cut advantage, with automated analysis and detailed graphical representation of the student feedbacks.
    •  
  • Attendance: This is a direct indicator of the percentage of the classes that a student has attended. This is obtained from individual departments and later is updated on to the edwisely data. This is used as parameter to understand the correlation of his performance and the percentage of classes attended by a student.
