Preface

The Report is a summary of performance during the course of your studies. The report is generated using a sophisticated learning model which incorporates key parameters of learning which are essential for your development as a professional.

The frndzzy application employs heuristic learning for students through mobile-learning which makes it highly customized to you because you are directly engaged in the application. The Outcome of Heuristic learning in students is the development of :
  • Self- learning
  • Critical thinking
  • and Logical thinking
Throughout the report, Graphs indicate progress made during the Semester in various aspects. Some key definitions to help navigate the report:

Learning (Model): The process of gaining knowledge through various activities, and sources. Learning can be measured from Academic scores, and new knowledge gained during the course-work.

How to read the report

  • Overview: Describes the overall performance till date with a holistic approach
  • Academics: Presents your Academic score
  • Questionnaire Analysis: Represents your semester wise analysis on questionnaires you took
  • Feeds: Its says about the number of knowledge feeds you have gone through till date
  • Time Spent: Its an indicator of the time you spent on the application
  • Response: It reflects your success rate in the questionnaires taken
  • Technology Utilization: This section quantifies your digital engagement throughout your studies
  • Learning: This gives gives a numerical measure of your learning using a proprietary model of edwisely
  • Overall Subject Mapping: Divides your entire curriculum into 4 broad domains and maps your competence to give your a picture of your strengths and weaknesses
  • Core Subject Mapping: Groups the core subjects of your study into 5 domains and gives recommendations on specific
  • Impact: This shows the correlation between technology utilization and academic performance
  • Industry Readiness: It gives you set of recommendations based on your entire profile on roles and companies suitable and also scope for improvement to become industry ready
